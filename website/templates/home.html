Frequently Asked Questions

How do I link my bank account for DBT?
Go to the "Financial Services" section in the dashboard. Select "Link Bank Account," enter your account number and IFSC code. Verification will take 24-48 hours.
Is my data secure on JanSetu?
Yes, JanSetu uses 256-bit encryption and is compliant with the National Data Security Policy. Your personal data is only shared with specific departments upon your authorization.
How to report a civic issue?
Click on the orange "Grievance" circle in the Quick Access menu. Select the category (Pothole, Garbage, Streetlight), upload a photo, and submit. You will receive a tracking ID instantly.
