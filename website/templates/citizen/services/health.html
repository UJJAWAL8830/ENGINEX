Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?
Use the "Book an Appointment" section above. Select your City and Specialty, then click Search. You can view available slots and click "Book Visit" to confirm.
Is the Ayushman Bharat card mandatory?
It is not mandatory for general treatment, but it is required to avail free treatment up to ₹5 Lakhs under the government scheme at empanelled hospitals.
Can I cancel my ambulance request?
Yes, you can cancel by calling 108 immediately if the emergency is resolved. However, misuse of emergency services is a punishable offense.
